Guide for the teacher

Once the course has been created indicating a number n of topics, a quiz composed of n questions is automatically created, linked to the course topics by scoring.

Each question adjusts the student's visualization of the related topic.

Warning: If the self-evaluation quiz is removed, recreate the course from the beginning.

Initial configuration

Once the course has been created, the teacher can:

  • Customize the topics created, changing the name and adding content and activities.
  • Customize the quiz questions, taking care not to change the scores.

    • Note: If necessary, manually reassign the scores follow the rules:
    • Question related to the topic 1 ➔ 20 pts
    • Question related to the topic 2 ➔ 21 pts
    • Question related to the topic 3 ➔ 22 pts
    • etc..


Addition of topics to the course

  • If the course has not yet been presented to the students or none of the students has yet attempted the quiz

    • The addition of a topic involves the automatic insertion of a question connected to it through the score.

    It is therefore necessary to customize the new question created

  • If the course has been presented to the students and there are attempts at the quiz

    • The new topic is not linked to the quiz and is visible to all students.


    Removal of topics from the course

  • If the course has not yet been presented to the students or none of the students has yet attempted the quiz

    • Removing a topic involves deleting a question from the quiz.

    Following the removal of the argument it is advised to check the maintenance of the question-topic link for all the questions.

  • If the course has been presented to the students and there are attempts at the quiz

    • Only topics not related to the quiz can be removed

    Attention!
    Do not remove topics related to the quiz: the topic is deleted but the quiz question is not deleted.


    Addition / removal of questions to the quiz

    The course format is topics-depending so the behavior is as follows:

  • Addition

    • The question once saved will be automatically removed

  • Removal

    • The question is removed, but a new default question will automatically be generated

    Note: If there are already attempts at the Moodle quiz, it does not allow modification to any question.
